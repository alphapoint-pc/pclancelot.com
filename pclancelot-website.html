About the Ministry

Meet PC Lancelot

PC Lancelot is a dedicated servant of God, called to intercede for people across the nations. Through the weekly "Bring Your Prayer Request" live stream on YouTube every Saturday at 9AM CST, this ministry has become a place of miracles, healing, and breakthrough for thousands of believers around the world.

What began as a simple desire to pray for people in the comment section of a YouTube video has grown into a global prayer movement — touching hearts from Nigeria to the United States, from the United Kingdom to the Philippines, and everywhere in between.

"The prayer of a righteous person is powerful and effective."

— James 5:16 (NIV)

This ministry believes in the power of agreement in prayer. When two or more gather in His name, He is there. Every request submitted is treated with reverence, confidentiality, and genuine faith.

Our Mission

To Be a House of Prayer for All Nations

The PC Lancelot Ministry exists to create a place of encounter — where ordinary people can bring their burdens before God and leave with the assurance that their prayers have been heard. We believe healing is real. Breakthroughs are available. And God is still in the business of answering prayer.
